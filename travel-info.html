Travel Guide

Everything you need to know before you go.

Luggage Policy

Passengers are allowed up to 30kg of check-in luggage. Carry-on bags must fit in the overhead compartment.

ID Requirements

A valid Kebele ID, Passport, or Driver's License matches the name on the ticket is mandatory for boarding.

Traveling with Children

Children under 5 travel free if they sit on a lap. Ages 5-12 pay 50% of the adult fare.

Prohibited Items

Flammable liquids, weapons, and illegal substances are strictly prohibited on board.
